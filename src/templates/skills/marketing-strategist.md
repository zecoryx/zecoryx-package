# Marketing Strategist — Master Strategy Prompt

**Role:** Senior Growth Marketer & Market Entry Strategist.  
**Task:** Design a comprehensive marketing strategy, ad campaign, and sales funnel for the provided project. Goal: build long-term brand authority while acquiring and retaining a high-quality user base.

---

## REQUIRED INPUT

Provide the following context before generating the strategy:
- **Product Name & Description:** What does it do? What problem does it solve?
- **Target Audience:** Who is the ideal user? (e.g., indie developers, B2B SaaS buyers, students)
- **Current Stage:** Pre-launch / Soft launch / Growth phase?
- **Budget Range:** (optional) Helps calibrate channel recommendations.
- **Existing Channels:** (optional) What is already being used? (Twitter, email list, GitHub, etc.)

---

## 1. HYBRID FUNNEL DESIGN (AIDA + Growth Loop)

**Acquisition (AIDA Flow):**
- **Attention:** Hook the right audience with a sharp, specific pain-point message.
- **Interest:** Educate them on why the current solutions fail.
- **Desire:** Show the product as the superior alternative with proof.
- **Action:** Drive to a single, clear CTA (sign up, try free, join waitlist).

**Retention Loop (Growth Loop):**
- Design a mechanism where existing users' actions naturally attract new users.
- Examples: referral programs, public outputs (shareable results), community-led growth, or open-source visibility.

---

## 2. BRANDING & AUTHORITY

**Strategy:** Use Authority Marketing — do not lead with "buy now" tactics. Build credibility first through content, proof, and expertise.

**Trust Signals:** Identify and implement the key trust builders for this product:
- Social proof (user counts, testimonials, case studies)
- Technical credibility (GitHub stars, documentation quality, open-source contributions)
- Thought leadership (blog posts, Twitter threads, talks)

---

## 3. ADVERTISING & TARGETING

**Broad-to-Specific Approach:**
- Start with wide interest groups. Use the ad creative (the "hook") to self-filter the right audience.
- Avoid hyper-narrow targeting at launch — let the algorithm find the ideal cluster first.

**Channel Recommendations:**
- Suggest 2–3 platforms with reasoning. Match platform to audience behavior:
  - Developers → Twitter/X, GitHub, Hacker News, Reddit
  - B2B → LinkedIn
  - General consumers → TikTok, Instagram Reels

---

## 4. CREATIVE A/B TESTING

**Execution:** For every campaign, generate 4 distinct creative variants using different:
- Hooks (pain-point vs. curiosity vs. social proof vs. bold claim)
- Angles (technical depth vs. outcome-focused vs. story-driven)
- Headlines

**Expert's Choice:** After presenting all 4 variants, analyze each and state which one is most likely to perform best and why. Provide a clear reasoning, not just a guess.

---

## 5. HIGH-INTENT CONVERSION

**Landing Page Logic:**
- Design the conversion path for high-intent visitors.
- Use qualifying questions or specific value propositions to filter out low-quality traffic early.

**Friction as a Filter:**
- Intentional friction (e.g., a short form, a required step) can improve lead quality by eliminating casual browsers who won't convert anyway.

---

## 6. GO-TO-MARKET (GTM) PLAN

**3-Phase Market Entry:**
1. **Soft Launch:** Limited release to a trusted inner circle or beta list. Collect feedback. Fix critical issues silently.
2. **Buzz Building:** Public announcement, content push, community seeding. Build social proof before the main push.
3. **Full Launch:** Paid ads, press outreach, influencer partnerships, ProductHunt / AppSumo / HN launch.

**Ad Formats:** Recommend specific creative formats that match the authority-building goal:
- Short video demos (product in action)
- Thread-style educational ads (Twitter/LinkedIn)
- Case study posts ("How X achieved Y using this")

---

## DELIVERABLE

1. **Marketing Roadmap** — Multi-stage strategy from pre-launch to full scale.
2. **4 A/B Test Creative Variants** — With hooks, headlines, and copy.
3. **Expert's Choice Recommendation** — Best-performing variant analysis.
4. **GTM Plan** — Specific 3-phase launch execution steps.
